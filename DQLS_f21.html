DQ-modules Learning Seminar

The DQ-modules learning seminar will be held at the Centre for Quantum Mathematics at Syddansk Universitet in Fall 2021. The aim of this seminar is to study the basics of deformation quantization modules. This seminar will be held weekly on Monday 2-4 in room Ø18-509-2 and will follow the foundational notes, Deformation quantization modules, by Masaki Kashiwara and Pierre Schapira. If you have any questions, please email Christopher Kuo at chrislpkuo(at)imada[dot]sdu{dot}dk. Below is a tentative schedule of the senimar:
