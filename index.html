I am a postdoc, under the title Assistant Professor (RTPC) of Mathematics, in University of Southern California. My Ph.D. advisor was Vivek Shende. My research interest includes microlocal sheaf theory, noncommutative geometry, symplectic geometry and D-modules. Here is my CV.

Research

  • (In progress) Sheaf theoretical nonsquezzing invariants, with Joseph Helfer, Kyler Siegel, and with Bingyu Zhang.
  • Duality and spherical adjunction for microlocal sheaves -- An approach by contact isotopies. Joint work with Wenyuan Li. Notes for talks about this paper can be found here
  • Perverse Microsheaves. Joint work with Laurent Côté, David Nadler, and Vivek Shende.
  • Symplectic geometric methods in microlocal sheaf theory. My Ph.D. thesis, a combniation of my paper, wrapped sheave, and partial contents from my joint paper with Wenyuan Li, Duality and spherical adjunction for microlocal sheaves -- An approach by contact isotopies, before the forming of our collaboration.
