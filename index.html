I am a postdoc, under the title Assistant Professor (RTPC) of Mathematics, in University of Southern California. I am on the job market this year, applying for academic positions. My Ph.D. advisor was Vivek Shende. My research interest includes microlocal sheaf theory, noncommutative geometry, symplectic geometry and D-modules. Here is my CV.

Research

  • Sheaf quantization of symplectic isotopies, in preparation. Joint work with Tomohiro Asano, Yuichi Ike, Wenyuan Li.
  • Integral transforms and compactness, in preparation. Joint work with Sheel Ganatra , Wenyuan Li, and Haosen Wu.
  • Homological combinatorics of Lagrangian coamoebae, in preparation. Joint work with Harold Williams.
  • Relative Calabi-Yau structure on microlocalization. Joint work with Wenyuan Li.
  • Duality, Künneth formulae, and integral transforms in microlocal geometry. Joint work with Wenyuan Li.
  • The microlocal Riemann-Hilbert correspondence for complex contact manifolds. Joint work with Laurent Côté, David Nadler, and Vivek Shende.
  • On the Hochschild cohomology of Tamarkin categories. Joint work with Vivek Shende and Bingyu Zhang.
  • Spherical adjunction and Serre functor from microlocalization. Joint work with Wenyuan Li. Notes for talks about this paper can be found here.
  • Perverse Microsheaves. Joint work with Laurent Côté, David Nadler, and Vivek Shende.
  • Symplectic geometric methods in microlocal sheaf theory. My Ph.D. thesis, a combniation of my paper, wrapped sheaves, and partial contents from my joint paper with Wenyuan Li, Duality and spherical adjunction for microlocal sheaves -- An approach by contact isotopies, before the forming of our collaboration.
  • Wrapped sheaves, Adv. Math 415 (2023): 108882. Notes for a talk given in USC is vailable here.
